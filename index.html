Tribute for Mac Miller

Mac Miller

aka Frank Sinatra

Here's a time line of Mac Miller's life:

  • 1992 - Born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  • 2007 - First album But My Mackin' Ain't Easy
  • 2010 - Realeased K.I.D.S.
  • 2011 - His single Donald Trump became his first song to chart on the US Billboard Hot 100
  • 2011 - He released a six-track EP, On and On and Beyond. This EP was his first entry into the US Billboard 200
  • 2011 - The album Blue Slide Park was released and reached 144.000 sales in its first week
  • 2012 - Miller released his seventh mixtape, Macadelic. The single Loud peaked at number 53 on the Billboard Hot 100
  • 2013 - Miller founded the record label imprint REMember Music, named after a deceased friend
  • 2013 - The album Watching Movies with the Sound Off was released
  • 2013 - He released the live album Live from Space
  • 2015 - GO:OD AM was released It charted at number four on the Billboard 200
  • 2016 - The Divine Feminine album debuted at number two on the Billboard 200 and number one on Billboard's Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums
  • 2018 - The album Swimming was released and nominated for Best Rap Album at the 61st Annual Grammy Awards
  • 2018 - Dies on September 7 at the age of 26 years

If you have time, you should read more about this genius on his Wikipedia entry.
